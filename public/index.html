Help & Guide
Welcome to AI Vision Assistant
This application uses advanced computer vision AI to help visually impaired users navigate their surroundings safely. The system automatically detects objects, obstacles, and hazards, providing real-time voice guidance.
Key Features
Automatic Object Detection
The AI continuously scans your surroundings using the camera and identifies objects, obstacles, and hazards in real-time.
Voice Alerts
Receive instant spoken notifications about detected objects, including their type and distance from you.
Multi-Modal Notifications
Get alerts through voice, sound, and vibration to ensure you never miss important information.
Customizable Settings
Adjust voice volume, speech speed, and language preferences to suit your needs.
How to Use
1
Allow Camera Access
Grant permission for the app to access your device camera when prompted.
2
Position Your Device
Hold your device at chest level, pointing forward to scan the area ahead of you.
3
Listen for Alerts
The AI will automatically detect objects and provide voice guidance about your surroundings.
4
Customize Settings
Visit the Settings page to adjust voice preferences, language, and notification options.
Important Safety Information
  • This app is an assistive tool and should not replace traditional mobility aids like canes or guide dogs.
  • Always use caution when navigating, especially in unfamiliar environments.
  • Ensure your device is fully charged before extended use.
  • Use headphones or earbuds in noisy environments for clearer voice guidance.
